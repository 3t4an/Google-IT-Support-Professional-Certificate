## What I learnied

I learned that the history of computers was built up of many advancements, which were the work of many extraordinary people, that collectively created the stairway to modern computing. It first started with mechanical machines that could calculate smaller numbers.
However, these were only limited to addition and subtraction. Someone had also created a programmable loom which took punch cards, read the holes, and operated accordingly to the "script." This set the foundation for programmable machines which could automate tasks
at a much higher than before. People then began combining these two innovations to create a programmable calculator. Computers had then evolved calculate trajectories and complex math for the military during WW2. These, however, took up the space of an entire room.
Companies like IBM and Apple had later developed computers that could fit on a desk. Apple had then gone on to create a portable computer, like the iPod. As technology advance and time went one, Apple continues to create better technology and now we have portable
devices like the iPhone 17.

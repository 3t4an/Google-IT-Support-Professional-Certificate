## What I Learned

1. **What is IT?** - I learned that IT is the general use of digital technologies that can store and process information. IT is the backbone of modern-day society and it so integrated in our daily lives, those that lack digital literacy are at a disadvantage.
IT is a large field of topics that can be mainly categorized under that following: Hardware & Software, Networking, Troubleshooting, Security, Scripting, AI, and Operating Systems.
2. **Digital Divide** - The Digital Divide is a fine line between those that **do** and **don't** have digital literacy. Those that do have access to a plethora of knowledge and tools to keep them going in their daily needs. Those that don't often
are at a disadvantage simply because they lack the skills of a basic, common utility.
3. **What does an IT Support Specialist do?** - I also learned about what an IT Support Specialist does. They ensure that the technological processes of an organization are running properly. This means Installing, Troubleshooting, Managing, Maintaining, and
Configuring
